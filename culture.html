Our Culture

At Phuthehang Group, we live by "People First". Innovation, respect, and work-life balance are not just words — they are how we work every single day.

Our Core Values

  • 🌟
    Innovation
    We reward bold ideas and celebrate continuous improvement at every level of the organisation.
  • 🤝
    Respect & Inclusion
    Every voice matters. Our diversity is our greatest strength and we honour it every day.
  • 🏆
    Excellence
    We deliver world-class service with integrity and hold ourselves to the highest professional standards.
  • ❤️
    Wellbeing
    Your health and happiness come first. A thriving employee builds a thriving company.

Our People

Our Mission

To empower every employee at Phuthehang Group with the resources, support, and environment they need to achieve their full professional potential — and in doing so, drive sustainable growth for Lesotho.

Our Vision

To be Lesotho's most admired employer — a workplace where talent is nurtured, diversity is celebrated, and every person feels they truly belong and contribute to something meaningful.
